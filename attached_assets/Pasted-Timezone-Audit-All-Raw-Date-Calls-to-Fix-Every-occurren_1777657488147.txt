Timezone Audit — All Raw Date Calls to Fix
Every occurrence of new Date(...).toLocaleString(), .toLocaleDateString(), .toLocaleTimeString()
that bypasses the user's saved timezone. Replace with useSettings() formatters.

1. frontend/src/pages/Campaigns.jsx
Add useSettings hook at the top of the component:
jsxconst { formatDateTime, formatDate } = useSettings();
Line A — scheduled time (uses .toLocaleString())
jsx// BEFORE
{new Date(c.scheduled_time).toLocaleString()}

// AFTER
{formatDateTime(c.scheduled_time)}
Line B — created_at (uses .toLocaleDateString())
jsx// BEFORE
{c.created_at ? new Date(c.created_at).toLocaleDateString() : '-'}

// AFTER
{c.created_at ? formatDate(c.created_at) : '-'}
